New Zealand Highlights Itinerary

Post by Admin on Wednesday, September 09, 2015 5 comments

You’ll experience the sunny beaches of Nelson before exploring the glaciers and forests of the wild West Coast. Explore New Zealand’s adventure capital Queenstown and visit the truly majestic Fox glacier. Enjoy the city scenary in Dunedin. Driving in New Zealand can be very different to driving at home. Before you set out on this fantastic journey, make sure you are familiar with driving in New Zealand.
